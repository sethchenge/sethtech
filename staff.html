history The SethChenge Story

Founded in 2019, SethChenge emerged from Seth's vision to bridge the gap between complex IT solutions and business needs. Starting as a solo entrepreneur with just a laptop and unwavering determination, Seth faced numerous challenges in the competitive IT landscape.

The early days were marked by long nights debugging systems, learning new technologies, and building trust with the first clients. The biggest challenge came in 2020 when the pandemic forced rapid digital transformation, but this became SethChenge's breakthrough moment.

Today, SethChenge has grown from a one-person operation to a trusted IT solutions provider, having successfully completed over 1000 projects and earned certifications from industry leaders like CISCO, AWS, and Microsoft.
